Purpose & Diversity

Welcome to
Leaders' Church International

Leaders' Church International (LCI) is a church with the vision of a multi-ethnic and demographically diverse church community which empowers a diverse workforce to effectively minister to the needs of diverse people groups in a socio-culturally diverse environment.

Born out of more than three decades of church pioneering experience, LCI mirrors the doctrine of Christ — a house of prayer for all nations (Isaiah 56:7, Matthew 21:13) and the demography of the first Church on the day of Pentecost.

We are the home of "BLESSED OVERCOMERS" — those whom faith in Christ qualifies as both blessed (Galatians 3:9) and overcomers (1 John 5:4).

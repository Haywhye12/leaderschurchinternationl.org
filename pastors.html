Apostle Israel Eledan

President & Overseer · Leaders' Church International

Apostle Israel Eledan is the Presiding Apostle overseeing Leaders' Church International. He answered the call of God into the ministry on the 30th of December 1982.

He has been travelling from coast to coast since then, advancing the gospel of Christ across nations. With a ministry spanning over four decades, Apostle Israel is a seasoned apostolic voice whose impact resonates across continents.

He is also the President and Founder of Valiant Men International and Israel Eledan Apostolic Vanguard — movements dedicated to equipping men and women for prophetic and apostolic ministry at the highest level.

An inspiring writer and sought-after speaker, Apostle Israel is an expert on leadership and organization development. He teaches the Word of God with a prophetic unction and apostolic breakthrough impact, drawing thousands to encounter the living God through his ministry.

He is also the Chairman of Grace Relief Initiative International, a humanitarian organization extending compassion and practical aid to vulnerable communities.

Rev. Grace Eledan

Vice-President & Co-Pastor · Leaders' Church International

Rev. Grace Eledan is the Vice-President of Leaders' Church International. She is a writer and an Evangelist by calling, also operating prophetic and healing gifts.

Rev. Grace carries a remarkable anointing for evangelism and healing ministry. Her involvement in church pioneering work spans over 20 years, during which she has been instrumental in building vibrant communities of faith across regions.

She is the President and Founder of Women Aflame International — a vibrant non-denominational ministry reaching women from all walks of life with the transformative love of Christ. Through this movement, countless women have found healing, purpose, and spiritual empowerment.

Together with Apostle Israel, she is the Co-Founder of Grace Relief Initiative International, channelling compassion and resources to serve the most vulnerable members of society. Her ministry embodies the heart of Christ — to see, serve, and restore.
